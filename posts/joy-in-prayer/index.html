Finding Joy in the Discipline of Prayer

Binu Mathew Thu Apr 02 2026 05:30:00 GMT+0530 (India Standard Time)

പ്രാർത്ഥന ഒരു ബാദ്ധ്യതയല്ല, ദൈവഹൃദയത്തോടുള്ള ആഴമേറിയ സൗഹൃദമാണ്.

When we pray, we are not informing God of things he doesn't know — we are joining him in his own sovereign purposes.
